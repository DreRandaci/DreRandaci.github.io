Background

Originally in business management for specialty coffee, I worked my way into upper management for a few years running my own cafe and indulging in cafe culture. Eventually, through my experience running a small business, I came across an opportunity to join a startup to combine my love for coffee with eCommerce to develop a webstore for imported Italian coffee equipment. Based out of Nashville, TN, the company aimed to become the main distributor for the entire south-east region. I helped build the webstore using Squarespace and Shopify, user friendly technologies with immense capabilities. That role ignited my interest in software development to learn and understand how these tools work under the hood and whether I could build some myself. After a few months of self-study through various online resources, I heard about Nashville Software School which offered a condensed, bootcamp-style education teaching the primary skills in coding, SCRUM methodology and the importance of further education in an industry where skill needs can change with the blink of an eye. I attended a few info sessions to learn more about the school, signed up for a Jumpstart class which was a month long introduction to web development, and quickly became hooked. I joined Evening Cohort E6 for front-end in June of 2017 and Day Cohort 22 in January of 2018 for Back-end. Now I'm looking forward to joining a team in my first role as a Junior Developer building automated tools that help solve the problems faced in the complexities of software development.
